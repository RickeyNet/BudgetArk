## About BudgetArk

BudgetArk is an offline-first personal finance app for debt tracking, monthly budgeting, and investment projections.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is my data stored?

BudgetArk stores your data locally on your device.

### What happens if I uninstall the app?

Uninstalling may remove local app data unless you exported a backup first.

### How do I back up my data?

Open **Profile** in the app and use **Export Data**.

### How do I restore my data?

Open **Profile** and use **Import Data**, then select your backup file.

### How do I change currency formatting?

Open **Profile** -> **Currency & Locale** and select your preferred option.

### I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email support with:

- device model
- OS version
- app version
- steps to reproduce
- screenshots (if available)
